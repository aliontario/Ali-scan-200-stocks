    How to trade index changes: Announcement comes out after market close → effective date is ~5 trading days later → passive ETFs (SPY, QQQ) are forced to buy additions and sell deletions on that date → the move happens in those 5 days.

    Buy calls on additions right after announcement. Short or buy puts on deletions. Additions typically move +3–10%, deletions –5–15% in that window. Data is saved in your browser and persists across sessions.
